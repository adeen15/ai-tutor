AI Tutor ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights as a parent or guardian.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Parent Email Address: Used solely for account login and account recovery. We do not send marketing emails without explicit consent.
  • Encrypted Password: Stored as an industry-standard hash (bcrypt). We never see or store your plain text password.
  • Child's First Name & Age Range: Used to personalize lessons and adjust difficulty level. We do not collect the child's full name, date of birth, or school.
  • Learning Progress Data: Coins, XP, quiz scores, lesson history, and inventory items — stored to preserve your child's progress across devices.
  • Device & Usage Data: Crash reports and anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. This data cannot identify individual users.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT store voice recordings. Audio is transcribed and immediately discarded.
  • We do NOT store photos or camera images. Images are analyzed in real-time and discarded within seconds.
  • We do NOT collect GPS location, physical address, or precise geolocation data.
  • We do NOT use advertising SDKs, behavioural tracking, or third-party ad networks.
  • We do NOT use your child's conversations to train any AI model.

4. Children's Privacy — COPPA & GDPR-K Compliance

This app is designed for children under 13. We comply with COPPA and GDPR-K.
  • We require verifiable parental consent (math-based parental gate) before any data is collected.
  • No AI conversation data is used for training. All AI inference is stateless.
  • All content is filtered through a dual-layer child safety system before being shown to a child.

5. Data Storage, Security & Retention

  • Storage: Supabase (AWS us-east-1) with row-level security.
  • Encryption: TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest.
  • Retention: Account data kept while account is active. Deleted within 48 hours on request.
  • Crash logs: Retained 90 days, then auto-deleted.

6. Parental Rights

  • Access: Review your data by emailing us.
  • Correct: Update account info within app settings.
  • Delete: Permanent deletion within 48 hours on request.
  • Withdraw Consent: Delete your account at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format.
