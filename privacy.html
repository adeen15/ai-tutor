Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

STRICT NO DATA SALE POLICY: We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your child's personal identification information to others. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Parent Email: Used solely for account recovery and login.
  • PIN Code: Encrypted and used for parental controls.
  • Progress Data: Coins, inventory, and learning stats to save your child's progress.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT store voice recordings. Voice inputs are processed instantly and discarded. We do not collect GPS location or physical addresses.

3. AI & COPPA Compliance

AI Tutor is designed to be COPPA compliant. No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent to AI models for training. We use stateless inference APIs.

4. Data Deletion

Parents can delete their account and all associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@aitutor.app
