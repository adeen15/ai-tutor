Frequently Asked Questions
How do I reset my PIN?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot PIN?" and enter your parent email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. Still stuck? Email us.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play — not through us directly.

iPhone/iPad: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → AI Tutor → Cancel
Android: Google Play → Menu → Subscriptions → AI Tutor → Cancel

Your premium access continues until the end of the paid period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are processed by Apple or Google depending on where you purchased. If it was recent (within 48 hours), you can request directly:

Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
Google: Google Play refund policy

You can also email us and we'll do our best to help.
Is my child's data safe?
Yes — completely. We are COPPA compliant. We never sell your data to anyone, ever. We don't store voice recordings, we don't collect your location, and we don't use advertising trackers. Read our full Privacy Policy.
How do I switch to a different child profile?
Tap the 👤 icon in the top-right corner of the app. You can add a new child profile or switch between existing ones from there. Each profile has its own name, age range, coins, and learning progress.
Premium isn't working after I bought it
This can happen if there was a delay in the purchase being confirmed. Try:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Log out and log back in
3. If on iOS, go to Settings → App Store → tap your Apple ID → Restore Purchases

Still not working? Email us with your account email and we'll fix it manually.
How do I delete my account and all data?
You can request full account deletion (as required by GDPR/COPPA) by emailing us. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours and confirm via email.
What age groups does AI Tutor support?
AI Tutor supports children ages 4 to 13. When you set up the app, you select your child's age range and the AI automatically adjusts the difficulty of questions, vocabulary, and explanations to match their level.
